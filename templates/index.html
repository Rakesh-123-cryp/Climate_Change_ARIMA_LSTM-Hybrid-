Overview

Our main objective is to help the government build an AI/ML model that predicts the occurrences of heat waves and the Air Quality Index (AQI) for the cities of Adilabad, Nizamabad, Warangal, Karimnagar, and Khammam.

Heatwaves and poor air quality have been major concerns in Telangana in recent years. The state experiences severe heatwaves during the summer months. These heatwaves not only pose a threat to public health, but also lead to a rise in air pollution levels, as people resort to using air conditioning and other cooling appliances that emit greenhouse gases. The Air Quality Index (AQI) in Telangana often reaches unhealthy levels during these periods, causing respiratory problems and other health issues. It is essential for the government and individuals to take proactive measures to mitigate the effects of heatwaves and improve air quality in the state.
